## What to build

Let a second anonymous player open a reusable room URL, join as the second participant, and prevent additional visitors from becoming participants.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] A second anonymous player can join an existing reusable room URL.
- [ ] The second anonymous player is assigned the open participant side.
- [ ] Both participants can see that the room is ready to play.
- [ ] A third visitor sees a full-room state.
- [ ] A third visitor cannot write participant state.
- [ ] Reopening the room as an existing participant preserves that participant's side.
